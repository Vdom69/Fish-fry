Advice For Your Fish

Discover more about fish before you buy

When it comes time to buy fish, take some time to research exactly what you are buying. Understand how big the fish will be, its temperament, space requirements and which fish it will get along with.

Watch for changes in fish and aquarium

Take a few minutes to observe the aquarium regularly. Watch for changes in the appearance, behavior or appetite of fish so that you immediately notice that something is wrong.

Avoid overfeeding fish

Do not feed more than the fish will eat in a few minutes. Excess food is not only harmful to fish and can lead to disease, but it will contaminate the aquarium and can cause splashes of the above chemicals.

Aquarium plants

Thanks to aquarium plants for fish in the aquarium conditions are close to natural. Aquarium plants, along with bacteria and the simplest organisms living in the aquarium, are involved in the cycle of substances: they clean the water from the products of life that are constantly formed in the water and harm fish, and aquarium plants, on the contrary, even need for normal growth. All types of plants are important for keeping fish.

Advice For Plants

Light

Lighting should be immediately after planting. In the beginning, daylight should be kept at 4-5 hours. Some still shine for 5-6 hours. Then there are fewer problems with algae fouling, plants grow more slowly but steadily.

Soil

The soil in the lawn aquarium has several functions. In addition to decorative and substrate for planting, it is an element of the entire biological filtration system. With good circulation, water passes through the soil, where it is purified by beneficial bacteria.

Water

Most plants like soft or medium hard water (dH 6-10 °) with a pH level of 6.5 to 7.5. The average temperature is 24 ° C.For simple and unpretentious plants, tap water is also suitable, which can be defended for a day before.
